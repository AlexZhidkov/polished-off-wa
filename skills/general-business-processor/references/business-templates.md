# General Business Email Templates

Templates for supplier, staff, payment, and sensitive business communications.

---

## Template 1: Supplier Communication

**Use when:** Communicating with suppliers  
**Tags:** `#supplier` `#procurement`  
**Risk:** Low

---

Hi [Name],

I'm following up on [order/quote/pricing] for [materials/description].

Could you please confirm:
• Current pricing for [items]
• Availability and lead times
• Delivery timeframe to [location]

We need these by [date] for an upcoming project. Please let me know if there are any constraints.

Thanks,
Val

---

## Template 2: Timeline Update (Conservative)

**Use when:** Providing schedule information  
**Tags:** `#scheduling` `#timeline`  
**Risk:** Low

---

Regarding start dates:

Our current schedule has availability around [timeframe]. However, start dates are estimates and subject to:
• Slab condition and preparation requirements
• Weather conditions (for external work)
• Current workload completion

We confirm scheduling once scope is finalised and a deposit is received.

## Template 3: Payment Follow-up

**Use when:** Following up on outstanding payment  
**Tags:** `#payment` `#accounts`  
**Risk:** Medium

---

Hi [Name],

I'm writing to follow up on invoice #[number] dated [date] for $[amount].

This invoice is now [X days/weeks] overdue. Could you please advise when payment will be processed?

If there are any issues with the invoice or work completed, please let me know so we can resolve them promptly.

Regards,  
Val

---

## Template 4: Complaint Response (High Risk)

**Use when:** Responding to quality/issue complaint  
**Tags:** `#complaint` `#quality` `#damage`  
**Risk:** CRITICAL — Draft only, do not send without review

---

Hi [Name],

Thank you for bringing this to our attention.

We take all concerns seriously and would like to understand the situation better. Could you please provide:
• Photos of the area in question
• Date the work was completed
• Description of the specific concern

Once we have this information, we can assess and advise next steps.

Regards,  
Val

---

**⚠️ Review Carefully — Sensitive Matter**

**Risk factors to consider:**
- Do not admit fault or liability
- Do not offer compensation without approval
- Document all communications
- Consider site visit to assess
- Escalate if legal language present

**Use when:** Internal team communication

**Risk:** Medium

• [Action item 1]

Regards,  
Val

## Template 6: Legal/Threat Response (High Risk)

**Use when:** Email contains legal language or threats  
**Tags:** `#legal` `#threat` `#urgent`  
**Risk:** CRITICAL — Draft only, escalate immediately

---

Hi [Name],

We have received your correspondence dated [date].

We are reviewing the matters raised and will respond in due course.

Regards,  
Val

---

**⚠️ Review Carefully — Sensitive Matter**

**CRITICAL:**
- Do not send without legal review
- Preserve all documentation
- Do not admit liability
- Escalate to appropriate advisor immediately
- Document timeline of events

## Template 2: Solid Colour vs Flake Explanation

**Use when:** Client asks about finish options or needs education on epoxy types  
**Tags:** `#education` `#finish-options` `#comparison`

---

**DRAFT RESPONSE**

[Category: Client Enquiry – Epoxy Flooring]  
[Risk Level: Low]

Hi [Name],

There are two main finish options for epoxy flooring:

**Solid Colour Epoxy (Most Cost-Effective)**
• Diamond grind + primer + 2 coats epoxy
• From $50 + GST per m²
• Clean, seamless look
• Designed for vehicle traffic (garages)
• Practical and easy to maintain
• Note: Can show slab marks more readily than flake systems

**Flake System (From $60 + GST per m²)**
• Provides a seamless polished-concrete / sandstone-style finish
• Modern architectural appearance
• Highly durable and easy to clean
• More depth and character than plain solid colour
• Best for hiding imperfections
• Gives an "aggregate-style" finish

**Our Recommendation:**
While solid colour epoxy works well in garages, we generally don't recommend it in living areas. It can appear flat and tends to show wear more readily. Flake systems offer a more refined look and hide marks far better over time.

Let me know your preference and we can guide you further.

Regards,

## Template 8: "Epoxy Paint" Clarification

**Use when:** Client mentions "epoxy paint" – education opportunity  
**Tags:** `#education` `#epoxy-paint` `#clarification`

---

**DRAFT RESPONSE**

[Category: Client Enquiry – Epoxy Flooring]  
[Risk Level: Low]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for your enquiry.

Just a quick clarification: when people refer to "epoxy painting", they usually mean a solid colour epoxy coating. That's one style of finish.

A **professional epoxy floor**, however, is a multi-layer system—not just paint. A proper installation includes:

1. **Mechanical preparation** – Diamond grinding the slab
2. **Primer/base coat** – Penetrates and bonds to the concrete
3. **Decorative layer** – Solid colour, flake, etc.
4. **Protective topcoat** – Seals the system, UV-stable where required

The decorative layer determines the final appearance, performance, and durability.

We offer both solid colour finishes (from $50 + GST per m²) and flake systems (from $60 + GST per m²).

Would you like to discuss which option might suit your project best?

Regards,  
Val

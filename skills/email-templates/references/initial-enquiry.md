**Use when:** New enquiry arrives

Hi [Name],

Thanks for getting in touch.

Before we provide an estimate, could you please help with a few quick details:

• Is this a new build or existing slab?
• What is currently on the floor (tiles, carpet, bare concrete)?
• Slab condition – Are you aware if the concrete has previously been painted, sealed, or coated (even lightly)?
• Flooring removal – Will the carpet and underlay be removed prior to our works, or would you like us to include this in the quote?
• Approximate total area (m²)?
• Your location?
• When are you hoping to complete the works?
• Do you have a preference for finish (nil, minimal exposure / full exposure, epoxy)?

If possible, please send a few photos and a floor plan. This helps us assess the slab and provide accurate advice. You can also refer to the brochure attached and the example links below to see different exposure levels.

Looking forward to your reply.

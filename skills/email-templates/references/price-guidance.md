As a general estimate:

• Nil exposure mechanical polish – approx $100/m² + GST
• Full exposure mechanical polish – approx $140/m² + GST
• High-end grind & seal – approx $95/m² + GST
• Standard grind & seal – approx $70/m² + GST

Final pricing depends on slab condition, preparation required, and total area.

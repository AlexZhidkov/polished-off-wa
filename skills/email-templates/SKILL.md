# Email Templates for Polished Off WA

Reusable email templates for consistent, professional client communication.

## When to Use

- Drafting responses to new enquiries
- Explaining finishes and options
- Providing pricing guidance
- Following up with stalled enquiries
- Coordinating site visits

## Templates Available

See `references/` folder for individual templates:

1. **initial-enquiry.md** — First response to new client enquiries
2. **exposure-explanation.md** — Explaining stone/aggregate visibility options
3. **mpc-vs-grind-seal.md** — Comparing mechanical polish vs grind & seal
4. **price-guidance.md** — Providing pricing estimates
5. **test-grind-offer.md** — Offering paid sample test grind
6. **renovation-tile-removal.md** — Explaining tile removal implications
7. **shower-area-policy.md** — Policy on wet areas
8. **follow-up.md** — Re-engaging stalled enquiries
9. **site-visit.md** — Offering on-site assessment

## Usage

Reference these templates when drafting emails. Adapt as needed for each specific situation while maintaining the core structure:

1. Acknowledge
2. Core response
3. Clear next step
4. Professional closing

## Tone Guidelines

- Professional, friendly, calm
- Direct and confident
- Educational (explain technical terms)
- No emotional language
- No over-apologising

## Company Positioning

**We compete on:**
- Quality finish
- Finish consistency
- Professional execution
- Long-term durability

**We do NOT compete purely on price.**
